Polished wants everyone to receive teeth cleanings with out insurance limitations. Most dental insurance companies do no recognize RDHAP's as providers therefore limiting their patients on what treatment can be received and who can treat them. At Polished, we feel everyone has a right to see anyone for treatment, anywhere so we are a fee for service practice. Offering affordable pricing to patients and their families.
