We offer dental hygiene services on site or in a patient’s home. Our goal is to bridge the gap for people who have limited or no access to dental hygiene care. As licensed registered dental hygienists in alternative practice we are able to treat patients who are unable to receive care in a traditional dental office setting. It no longer matters if the patient is home bound, has a mental or physical disability, severe dental phobia, or living in skilled nursing or assisted living facilities, each and every person deserves access to dental hygiene care and we are here to provide it.
