How It Works: Getting started with Polished is easy. You can reach out to us via phone, email, our website, or simply click the link for new patients. Once we receive your details, we will contact you to discuss your specific needs and explore how we can best assist you. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to get in touch with our team. We are here to help. What to Expect During Your First Visit: The initial visit includes an oral hygiene assessment, an oral cancer screening, a teeth cleaning, a fluoride treatment, and personalized oral hygiene instructions tailored to the patient's unique needs. This appointment lasts approximately 60 minutes, allowing us ample time to address your concerns and provide the care you deserve. Before scheduling any further treatment, the RDHAP will discuss the recommended treatment plan with you or your healthcare guardian. Our goal is to align our services with your individual wishes and preferences.
