How It Works: Getting started with Polished is easy. You can reach out to us via phone, email, our website, or simply click the link for new patients. Once we receive your details, we will contact you to discuss your specific needs and explore how we can best assist you. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to get in touch with our team. We are here to help.

What to Expect During Your First Visit: The initial visit includes an oral hygiene assessment, an oral cancer screening, a teeth cleaning, a fluoride treatment, and personalized oral hygiene instructions tailored to the patient's unique needs. This appointment lasts approximately 60 minutes, allowing us ample time to address your concerns and provide the care you deserve. Before scheduling any further treatment, the RDHAP will discuss the recommended treatment plan with you or your healthcare guardian. Our goal is to align our services with your individual wishes and preferences.

Teeth Cleanings

A gentle, thorough cleaning that removes plaque, tartar, and surface stains to keep your teeth and gums healthy. Recommended every six months for most patients.

Periodontal Maintenance

Specialized cleanings for patients with a history of gum disease. We remove buildup above and below the gumline to help control inflammation and protect the bone supporting your teeth.

Oral Hygiene Assessments

A complete look at your teeth, gums, and home-care routine. We identify any areas that need attention and build a plan that fits your goals and abilities.

Oral Cancer Screenings

A quick, painless visual and tactile exam of the lips, tongue, cheeks, and throat to look for any concerning changes. Early detection saves lives — included free with every cleaning.

Patient-Specific Oral Hygiene Instructions

One-size-fits-all advice rarely works. We coach patients (and caregivers) on brushing, flossing, and product choices tailored to mobility, dexterity, and individual needs.

Denture Cleaning

Professional cleaning that removes built-up tartar, stains, and bacteria from full and partial dentures. Helps dentures fit better, last longer, and feel fresh.

New Patient Appointment

A relaxed first visit (about 60 minutes) that includes a full assessment, oral cancer screening, cleaning, fluoride treatment, and a personalized care plan we'll review with you or your caregiver.

Topical Fluoride Application

A varnish that strengthens enamel and helps prevent decay — especially helpful for patients with dry mouth, exposed root surfaces, or limited home-care ability. Free with any cleaning.

Dental Referrals

If you need care beyond hygiene — fillings, extractions, dentures, or specialty treatment — we connect you with trusted local dentists and coordinate the next step on your behalf.
