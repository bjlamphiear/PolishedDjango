About Us

At Polished our mission is to provide high level oral hygiene care to everyone. Oral health is directly associated to overall health, therefore its vastly important to maintain your oral hygiene no matter what the circumstance. The population of patients who are not able to be seen in a dental office setting is growing yet there need for oral hygiene care is also growing so we are here to try to bridge that gap. Wethers its cleaning teeth, educating care givers with tips and tricks or even simply helping brush a patients teeth, we are here for you. While we have a list of services provided, we are always willing and wanting to help promote oral health so if you have a need and don’t see it listed please reach out.
