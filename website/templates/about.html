Our Mission

At Polished our mission is to provide high level oral hygiene care to everyone. Oral health is directly associated to overall health. The population of patients who are not able to be seen in a dental office continues to grow but access is no longer a barrier to good dental hygiene care. Whether its cleaning teeth, educating care givers with tips and tricks or even simply helping brush a patient's teeth, we are here for you.
